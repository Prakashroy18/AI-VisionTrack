📈 Next Steps

High School (10th SSC)

  • Focus on matriculation exams, fundamentals.
  • Explore streams: Science, Commerce, Arts.

Intermediate (MPC, Biotech, Commerce)

  • MPC: Prepares for engineering entrances like JEE, EAMCET.
  • Biotech: Leads toward medical or life sciences.
  • Commerce: Business and finance fundamentals.

Engineering (BTech)

  • Multiple specializations available.
  • Focus on projects, internships, certifications.

Post Graduate (MTech, MBA, MSc)

  • Advanced specialization and research.
  • Prepare for GATE, NET, other relevant exams.

Software Development Career

  • Learn programming, data structures, algorithms.
  • Gain experience in full-stack, DevOps, cloud.

Data Science Career

  • Master statistics, machine learning, AI.
  • Work on real datasets and projects.

Management Career

  • Develop leadership, team building skills.
  • Pursue MBA or equivalent to accelerate growth.

Entrepreneurship Path

  • Build ideas into products and services.
  • Learn business planning, funding, marketing.

📅 4-Year BTech Timeline

Year 1: Foundation Building

  • C/C++, Engineering Maths, Physics, Engineering Drawing
  • Target: 8.5+ CGPA
  • DSA basics (Arrays, Linked Lists)
  • College fests, tech clubs

Year 2: Core Subjects

  • OOPS, DBMS, OS, Computer Networks
  • First internship (2 months summer)
  • 2-3 projects on GitHub
  • Competitive programming

Year 3: Specialization

  • Core branch subjects + Electives
  • Off-campus internships (Tier-2/3)
  • AWS/Azure certifications
  • Hackathons, open source

Year 4: Placements

  • Campus placements (Tier-1/2/3)
  • Resume building, mock interviews
  • Off-campus (FAANG, startups)
  • Final year project

🚀 Entrance Exam Preparation

Daily Schedule (11th-12th)

  • 6 AM-12 PM: School/Coaching (PCM focus)
  • 2 PM-8 PM: Self-study (PCM focus)
  • 8 PM-10 PM: Practice/Revision (Foundation books)
  • 10 PM-12 AM: Mock tests & problem solving
  • Total: 10-12 hours daily

Resources

  • Books: NCERT (Physics), Foundation Mathematics (Maths), Standard Textbooks
  • Coaching: Reputed coaching centers, Online platforms
  • Tests: Mock test series, Previous year papers
  • Online: Educational websites, Video lectures, Practice platforms
